---
title: "Hana Nichtburgerová"
---
Hana Nichtburgerová, M.A., earned her Master's degree in Jewish Studies with a focus on literature, history, and philosophy at the Hochschule für Jüdische Studien in Heidelberg. Since 2015, she has been a member of the Centre for the Study of the Holocaust and Jewish Literature. She is a doctoral student at the Institute of Czech Literature and Comparative Studies, Faculty of Arts, Charles University. She has completed study stays abroad at the University of Amherst and at universities in Vienna, Heidelberg, and Hamburg. Since 2025, she has been part of the Czech node of the European Holocaust Research Infrastructure at the Masaryk Institute and Archives of the Czech Academy of Sciences.

Her research focuses on Holocaust literature, autobiography, and ego-documents. She has contributed to the publication Handbook of Polish, Czech, and Slovak Holocaust Fiction.
